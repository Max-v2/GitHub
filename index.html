Futuristic Version

I have created and trained an artificial intelligence model using images of myself as the foundation. The project started with gathering a series of photos of me from different angles and with various expressions to feed the model. Using advanced machine learning techniques, such as Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) or pre-trained models, I enabled the AI to accurately recognize and reproduce my face with high precision.

Once the model was fully trained, I set out to generate images based on creative prompts. One of the prompts I used was: "A futuristic version of myself, standing in a neon-lit city, wearing a sleek, high-tech suit with glowing elements, surrounded by towering skyscrapers and flying cars." With this description, the AI generated a new image, using my face as the base while adapting the surrounding elements to match the futuristic aesthetic described.

The process involved multiple stages, including data cleaning, image preprocessing, and carefully fine-tuning the model to ensure that the images were both accurate and creative. In the end, I created a system that fuses my visual identity with the power of AI to generate striking, one-of-a-kind images, blending reality with imagination.
